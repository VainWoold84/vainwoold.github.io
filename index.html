About Me

Hi, I'm VainWoold. I'm a 16-year-old French student with a burning passion for storytelling through digital art.

My journey started just a year ago. Balancing high school studies with late-night Blender sessions, I taught myself the intricacies of 3D modeling and the patience required for Stop Motion animation.

I don't just move pixels; I try to evoke emotion. My goal is to turn this hobby into a professional career in the animation industry.
